Source: [Office of the Registrar - Fee Schedule](https://registrar.berkeley.edu/tuition-fees/fee-schedule/)

The entire Berkeley Campus Fee is comprised of smaller campus-based fees. Each of these was voted on and approved by the UC Berkeley student body during a past election cycle.

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :fontawesome-solid-building: __Lower Sproul Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#1f77b4; color:white; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    Although it once went toward the construction of MLK and Eschleman Student Union, this fee now pays for some of the maintenance of those buildings.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](lower-sproul.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-hands-holding-circle: __Wellness Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#aec7e8; color:black; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    This fee goes toward RecSports, the University Health Center, and PATH2CARE. The discretionary portion of the fee funds other student organizations and campus departments each year.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](wellness.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-house-medical: __Health Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#ff7f0e; color:white; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    This covers the salaries and benefits of multiple staff positions at the University Health Center, and a few other services there.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](health-fee.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-users: __Big C Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#ffbb78; color:black; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    This fee funds the Centers for Educational Justice and Community Engagement (EJCE). It also funds student organization projects that target underserved campus populations each year.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](big-c.md)
    
-   :fontawesome-solid-wrench: __Life Safety Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#2ca02c; color:white; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    The Life Safety fee funds projects that keep the architecture of campus spaces safe for students, such as building renovations and seismic retrofitting.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](health-fee.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-utensils: __Basic Needs Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#98df8a; color:white; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    This fee goes toward the Basic Needs department under the Divison of Equity and Inclusion. It funds services related to food, housing, and financial security for students in need.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](basic-needs.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-graduation-cap: __GOLD Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#d62728; color:black; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    The GOLD Fee funds the Centers for Educational Equity and Excellence (CE3), which supports the success of non-traditional students at Berkeley. CE3 houses programs such as the Educational Opportunity Program (EOP), Transfer Student Center (TSC), and Student Parent Center (SPC).

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](gold.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-seedling: __TGIF Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#ff9896; color:white; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    TGIF goes toward the Student Environmental Resource Center. The fee also supports the Green Initiative Fund, which distributes grants to projects that promote sustainability on campus.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](tgif.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-newspaper: __V.O.I.C.E. Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#9467bd; color:black; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    This fee supports the DailyCal, UC Berkeley's student-run newspaper.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](voice.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-faucet: __Student Center Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#c5b0d5; color:white; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    Once funding renovations to the Dining Commons in the Student Union Complex, the Student Center Fee now pays for some of the maintenace and operations of MLK and Eschleman.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](student-center.md)


-   :fontawesome-solid-house: __Housing Security Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#8c564b; color:black; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    This fee goes towards the Housing Security Award Fund, which students can qualify for by applying to the Basic Needs Center's Emergency Fund.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](housing-security.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-earth-americas: __Ethnic Studies Fee__
    {: style="background-color:#c49c94; color:white; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    This fee goes towards the Ethnic Studies Department at UC Berkeley. Specifically, it funds the Ethnic Studies' 5th account, which distributes grants to student-initiated events for the Ethnic Studies Community.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](ethnic-studies.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-gavel: __ASUC Fee (Undergrad Students Only)__
    {: style="background-color:#e377c2; color:black; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    The ASUC Fee goes toward the undergraduate student government's internal budget, which includes senator stipends and ASUC chartered programs, as well as registered student organizations that apply through the ABSA application.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](health-fee.md)

-   :fontawesome-solid-scroll: __GA Fee (Grad Students Only)__
    {: style="background-color:#e377c2; color:black; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    The GA Fee goes toward the graduate student government's internal budget, as well as registered graduate student organizations.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](health-fee.md)


-   :fontawesome-solid-hand-fist: __STARR Fee (Undergrad Students Only)__
    {: style="background-color:#f7b6d2; color:black; padding:15px; border-radius: 25px;" }

    ---

    The STARR fee funds multiple programs, student, and full-time staff positions within the *bridges* Multicultural Resource Center on campus, which provides services related to recruitment and retention of first-generation low-income students of color.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Learn More](health-fee.md)



</div>
